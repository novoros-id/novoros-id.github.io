Magic Crystal Board Game

Do you like board games as much as we do? We love board games and strive to bring the spirit of the board game to the computer. This is a classic turn-based board game where each player rolls a die and moves his piece across the field. It is necessary to look for keys, open doors, fight enemies and, of course, defeat the boss. This game can be played both with a computer and with friends (up to 4 people). Specify who will play as a Knight, Magician, Barbarian or Priest, and pass the phone to each player so that he makes his magic move. There are 15 levels of training in the game, and they will always be free. In the future, there will be missions, a plot and an online game via a game server. Let the magic crystal help you! Have a nice game!
